Preparatory Studies & Skill Development

The Preparatory Studies and Skill Development Unit at ICST is dedicated to equipping students with the essential skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen degree programs and navigate the demands of the professional world. These programs are particularly beneficial for students requiring additional support in specific academic areas or those transitioning to a new educational system.

Tailored to meet individual student needs and the prerequisites of their intended fields of study, preparatory studies cover a diverse range of subjects. These commonly include English language proficiency, soft skills, technical competencies, and foundational courses pertinent to their chosen disciplines.

The duration of preparatory studies is flexible, spanning from several months to a full academic year, contingent upon program structure and student requirements. The primary objective is to ensure students possess a robust foundation and requisite skills to seamlessly progress into their regular degree programs.

To aid students throughout their preparatory studies, ICST offers a suite of support services, including academic advising, tutoring, and supplementary resources, all aimed at fostering student success. For further information, please do not hesitate to reach out to the ICST Admissions Office.
